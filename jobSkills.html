Preparing for the job market

During our boot camp, I am grateful to have an employability tutor available to us. Every week, we cover topics that are crucial for honing skills and increasing our chances of securing a job in the industry. In our first session, we discussed more than just job titles and salaries - it prompted me to think about elements like company ethos and values aligning with my own. It's essential for me to work in a place that has a positive culture and sparks my passion and interest.

We also talked about how to make the most of the upcoming weeks in boot camp while still maintaining balance in our daily lives. Let's face it, software boot camps can be intense with a heavy workload and high expectations. That's why it's important to be kind to yourself emotionally and mentally during this time. Personally, I remind myself of my motivation for signing up, stay committed, do my best, and maintain good habits throughout the course.
