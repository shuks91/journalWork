Week 3

Week 3 was dedicated to refining our journals and delving into Flexbox. With the help of the resources provided and a trusty companion - YouTube, the styling of my website began to take shape. We were given ample self-study time, allowing us to work on our design and content.

On Tuesday, in our alternate session, we collaborated in groups to delve into the Project life cycle, exploring various approaches to Agile. It was during this session that I was introduced to Scrumâ€”a project management framework centred around breaking down tasks into manageable sprints, allowing for focused planning and management one step at a time. In addition to this, I dedicated time to making a data schema for a case study on an archery app and designed the app's interface using PowerPoint.

Thursday saw us continue our venture into Project management. Within our groups we delved into the functionalities essential for our app, engaging in discussions and drafting user story cards for our opening menu. Additionally, we made a flow chart to visualise the steps a user would take on the landing page.

Friday presented us with a challenge, we were given an hour to tackle three codewars challenges of varying difficulty. While I succeeded in the first challenge, I encountered difficulties with the remaining two. Nevertheless, my attempts demonstrated my determination, and I am eager to revisit and successfully complete them. We also dedicated time to completing our project until 3:30 PM, after which we concluded the week with an employability session.



Looking ahead to week 4, the weeks have been flying by!
